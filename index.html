About Us
Who We Are
Peak Garage Solutions is a Burlington, Ontario-based business dedicated to helping Canadian homeowners take back their garage space with custom-built tote storage systems.

What We Build

We design and hand-build solid wood tote rack systems sized exactly to your garage and your needs. Choose anywhere from 1 to 5 columns wide and 2 to 5 rows high — every unit is cut, glued, and assembled to order. Add a plywood top, a stain finish, heavy-duty casters, or a full set of 27-gallon storage totes, and we'll build it all in.

Our Commitment

We believe garage storage should be tough, good-looking, and built to last. That's why every frame is glued solid wood construction — no particle board, no wobble. Our units are designed for real Canadian garages: they handle the cold, the weight, and the chaos of every season. We're proud to build locally and deliver throughout the Burlington and Hamilton area.
